TRUSTWORTHY CHARITY INTERNET-BASED PLATFORM IN VIETNAM

Our mission is Connecting trustful charity organizations and potential participants. Doing that, we aim to connect good souls to make a better society.

Connectivity

It is problematic to either NGOs, donors or volunteers to contact the other 2 groups to find suitable ones to their needs due to a shortage of a centralized source of information. Most of the time, all three groups tend to exploit their existing networks to seek suitable partners; however, their networks are limited which therefore limits their options. Our platform helps to connect networks to networks which will significantly expand their connectivity following geometric progression.

Transparency

This is the key obstacle discouraging all 3 groups to actively involved in a partnership. Imagine how difficult for a donor wanting to donate to a promising social project but is unable to verify the trustworthiness of such project, or a volunteer wanting to join a newly established social organization but is unable to find out if such organization is not a shadow company to cover some unlawful activities of other organization behind. We will develop enhanced identification and verification programs to check the background of the organizations as well as various assessment measures to supervise and review the reliability of the organizations or the effectiveness and efficiency of the projects.

Transformation

We play a role of consultant to provide well-tailored services to NGOs regarding their strategy and operation, for example, providing International NGOs with a suitable approach to establish or run its operation in another countries, helping local NGOs with project management and credibility establishment, or offering advisory services to corporates who want to start their CSR project.
